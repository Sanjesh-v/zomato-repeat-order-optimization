# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Zomato Smart Reorder Hub

## Executive Summary

The objective of this project is to increase Zomato's Repeat Order Rate by reducing decision fatigue during food selection.

Analysis indicates that users frequently reorder from previously visited restaurants but spend significant time browsing before placing an order. The proposed Smart Reorder Hub surfaces previous orders and favorite restaurants directly on the homepage, enabling faster ordering and improved retention.

---

# Problem Statement

Repeat purchases are critical for sustainable growth in food delivery platforms.

Current user behavior shows:

- High browsing activity
- Low repeat order conversion
- Excessive time spent choosing restaurants
- Frequent ordering from previously visited restaurants

This suggests that users face decision fatigue despite having established preferences.

---

# Business Goal

Increase Repeat Order Rate from:

42% → 48%

within 6 months of launch.

---

# Product Goal

Enable users to quickly reorder meals they already enjoy through a personalized homepage experience.

---

# Proposed Solution

## Smart Reorder Hub

A personalized homepage section allowing users to quickly reorder previous meals.

### Components

#### Order Again

Displays last 5 orders.

#### Favorite Restaurants

Displays frequently ordered restaurants.

#### Personalized Suggestions

Recommendations based on:

- Time of day
- Previous orders
- Cuisine preferences

#### One-Click Reorder

Users can reorder directly from homepage.
